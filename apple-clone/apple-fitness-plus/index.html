    New

    Custom Plans that just get you.

    Jump right in with a workout and meditation schedule designed around you. Fitness+ automatically creates prebuilt Custom Plans based on your favorite activities. Use Get Started to help you build a routine, then keep it up with the Stay Consistent or Push Further plan or build your own. Find it all in the Plans page.4

    Get personalized recommendations in For You.

    From day one, Fitness+ gets to know you and what you like to do. And as you continue using it, youll see personalized recommendations based on your favorite activities, trainers, durations, and music.

    Metrics that keep you ontrack.

    Supercharge your experience with Apple Watch.

    Get real‑time metrics and live progress on
    your Activity rings to stay motivated while you workout.

    With AppleWatch, you can keep an eye on your metrics, like heart rate and Activity rings, in the moment. Youll also get postworkout insights to help you understand your progress. Along with exclusive audio‑guided walks, runs, andmeditations.

    New

    Real-time metrics from your AirPods Pro 3.

    Connect AirPods Pro 3, Powerbeats Pro 2, or any Bluetooth heart rate monitor to see personal stats, like calories burned, while youre working out. Heart rate tracking on these devices also enables the Burn Bar, which compares your effort to others who have done the same workout.1

    Questions? Answers.

    • You can find it in the Fitness app on iPhone in the second tab. On iPad, the Fitness app is available for users to download from the AppStore. The Fitness app is also available on AppleTV. On AppleWatch, Fitness+ subscribers can find Fitness+ Audio Workouts in the Workout app and Fitness+ Audio Meditations in the Mindfulnessapp.

    • No. All you need is an iPhone to use Fitness+. When you use Fitness+ with AppleWatch, youll get real‑time personal metrics onscreen, like your heart rate, the calories you burn, and the progress of all three Activity rings, during everyworkout.

    • When you use AirPods Pro 3 or Powerbeats Pro 2 with Fitness+, youll see your real-time personal metrics onscreen, including your heart rate and calories burned during the workout. You can also see these metrics when you wear a third-party heart rate monitor connected to your device.1

    • It depends on the workout you choose. While most dont require any equipment, for some you may need things like dumbbells, a resistance band, an indoor cycling bike, a treadmill, or a rowing machine. Other helpful equipment might include iPhone stands, a yoga mat, yoga blocks, or a meditation cushion. You can use any brand of equipment at home or in thegym.

    • Yes, you can use Fitness+ on Apple TV. Just connect your iPhone or Apple Watch to your Apple TV in the Fitness app. You can also stream Fitness+ workouts and meditations to AirPlay‑enabled TVs and devices. See here for compatibility details.

    • Yes. You can share AppleFitness+ with up to five family members. To get started, just set up FamilySharing on your iPhone or iPad and invite family members to join. Each family member gets their own personalized Fitness+ experience. See apple.com/family-sharing for moreinformation.

    • When you first subscribe to Fitness+, simply tap the activities you like to do. For You shows recommendations based on your selections. As you use Fitness+, ForYou updates based on your activities, and it even reflects your favorite trainers, durations, music, and equipment preferences.

    • Yes. Fitness+ gives you premade Custom Plans based on the activities, durations, and trainers you already enjoy in Fitness+. In the Plans page, youll see options for Stay Consistent, a schedule of workouts or meditations that matches your current routine, and Push Further, a plan that adds a longer workout or an extra workout to every week. If youre new to Fitness+, your Get Started plan provides a starting point based on your selected interests or popular activities.

      You can also build your own Custom Plan by selecting days, activities, durations, and trainers even your favorite music.

    • ForYou offers recommendations based on what you like to do in Fitness+. You can also choose an activity type and filter by trainer, duration, music, meditation theme, and more. When you know exactly what you want, go to Search and enter it to get matching results.

    • Yes. With AudioFocus, you can prioritize the volume of the trainer or the music so you can hear more of whatever is most important to you. Within a session, tap the more button and then Audio Focus to prioritize the trainer or the music.

    • Yes. Stacks allow you to select multiple workouts and meditations to do back to back, so you can move seamlessly from one activity to the next. When you complete an activity, youll see your next workout or meditation in the Summary tab, so its easy to keep the momentum going. To add a workout or meditation to a Stack, tap the more button on the episode detail page and choose Add to Stack.

    • AppleFitness+ is currently available in Argentina, Australia, Austria, Belgium, Bolivia, Brazil, Canada, Chile, Colombia, Costa Rica, Dominican Republic, Ecuador, El Salvador, Finland, France, Germany, Guatemala, Honduras, Hong Kong, Iceland, India, Indonesia, Ireland, Italy, Japan, Malaysia, Mexico, Netherlands, New Zealand, Nicaragua, Norway, Panama, Paraguay, Peru, Philippines, Poland, Portugal, Russia, Saudi Arabia, Singapore, Spain, Sweden, Switzerland, Taiwan, the United Arab Emirates, the United Kingdom, the United States, Venezuela, andVietnam.

    • If you purchased a new iPhone 8 or later, Apple Watch Series 3 or later, iPad, Apple TV, AirPods Pro 3, or Powerbeats Pro 2, you can try Apple Fitness+ free for three months. The offer can be redeemed on your iPhone or iPad. Youll need to update your iPhone and Apple Watch to the latest OS, and youll have three months to redeem the offer after the first activation of the eligible device.2
