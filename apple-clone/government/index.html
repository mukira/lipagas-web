    Government

    Next-level reliability, efficiency, and security. For every level of government.

    Next-level reliability. efficiency. security.
    For every level of government.

    Apple provides a powerful, flexible platform that helps government agencies do more for their communities. iPhone, iPad, Mac, Apple Watch, and Apple Vision Pro are secure, accessible, and sustainable by design, and they offer advanced features that transform workflows agencywide.

    Productivity from the office to the field and beyond.

    Apple products give teams the tools they need to be productive from anywhere they work. Built-in features enable employees in the field to easily access real-time information for more empowered decision-making.

    Apple devices run your go-to apps. Nearly 2 million third-party apps are compatible with Apple products, including Microsoft 365. It’s easy to build custom apps, too. And essential apps come built in to help you accomplish virtually any task.

    One powerfully connected ecosystem makes it simple to share files instantly and multitask with ease. Apple products are made to work together — employees can move seamlessly between devices to get more done.

    Success Stories

    Apple supports agencies around the world.

    Security and IT, seamless and streamlined.

    • Device setup

      that’s simple and secure — right out of the box.

      Device setup

      that’s simple and secure — right out of the box.

      Apple Business Manager works with MDM solutions like Jamf, Intune, and Workspace One to make device deployment and management easy, no matter the scale. And Automated Device Enrollment enables zero-touch deployment of Apple devices from the second they’re powered on.

    • Controls for IT

      manage security settings systemwide.

      Controls for IT

      manage security settings systemwide.

      Apple devices have built-in management and security frameworks that enable IT teams to configure devices, deploy apps, and monitor threats remotely. And IT can maintain full control over corporate devices while preserving privacy for employee-owned devices.

    • Built-in security

      protects against viruses and malware.

      Built-in security

      protects against viruses and malware.

      Powerful features protect users and devices, while built-in frameworks enable IT to manage security systemwide. Apple hardware and software are designed together to make advanced security seamless for users while conserving IT resources.

    • Automatic encryption

      keeps sensitive information safe.

      Automatic encryption

      keeps sensitive information safe.

      Apple devices have encryption features that safeguard user data, even when other parts of the security infrastructure have been compromised. These features benefit users and IT administrators, protecting personal and agency information and providing methods for instant and complete remote wipe in the case of device theft or loss.

    • Platform certifications

      help keep your agency compliant.

      Platform certifications

      help keep your agency compliant.

      Apple regularly engages with third-party organizations like FIPS and Common Criteria to certify and attest to the security of Apple’s hardware, software, and services. These internationally recognized organizations provide Apple with certifications for each major operating system release.

    • Endpoint Security

      allows IT to monitor threats.

      Endpoint Security

      allows IT to monitor threats.

      With Apple’s Endpoint Security framework, developers can build solutions that monitor, analyze, and respond to advanced security threats. And IT can stay on top of threats without affecting overall performance for users.

    Sustainable from start to forever.

    Apple has a plan to be carbon neutral by 2030. By focusing on recycled and renewable materials, clean electricity, and low-carbon shipping, Apple is working to bring its net emissions to zero across its entire carbon footprint.

    MacBook Air is now made with 55% recycled materials, including 100% recycled aluminum in the enclosure — without sacrificing quality. Apple products are designed, built, and tested for durability, so they last longer.

    Trade in your device for credit — or recycle it for free. When it’s time to upgrade, you can trade in current devices and apply the value to new ones, online or in the store. And if your device isn’t eligible, Apple will recycle it at no cost.

    Government should be
    accessible to everyone.

    That’s why our products and services have built-in features designed to meet your agency’s accessibility requirements.

    Innovations that speak volumes. Features like VoiceOver describe what’s on your screen, including in braille. And with Spoken Content, your Apple device can speak text out loud.

    Bigger, bolder, and clearer for you. Magnifier allows you to increase the size of anything you point your iPhone or iPad towards — and screen customizations make it easy to change display settings and text size.

    Navigate your devices — your way. Use Voice Control to navigate your device using voice commands. And get real-time captions of live conversations, audio from apps or any web content with Live Captions.
