    Get to know AppleWatch.

    • Health

      Knows you. Insightsandout.

      Health

      Knows you. Insights and out.

      Get notified of chronic high blood pressure.

      AppleWatch can spot signs of chronic high blood pressure and notify you of possible hypertension.3 How? The optical sensor provides data to an algorithm that can detect potential hypertension by analyzing how your blood vessels respond to beats of the heart over 30-dayperiods.

      You snooze. You win.

      The all-new sleep score helps you track and understand the quality of your sleep so you can make it more restorative. You can also use the Sleep app to set up a bedtime routine with Wind Down reminders. AppleWatch can even notify you if you show signs of possible sleepapnea.4

      Never miss a beat.

      With the ECG app, AppleWatch is capable of generating an ECG similar to a single-lead electrocardiogram.5 And the HeartRate app alerts you of high and low heart rates, as well as irregularrhythm.6

      Better understand your daily health status.

      The Vitals app can help you identify changes in your health.7 Quickly see your key overnight health metrics right on your wrist including heart rate, respiratory rate, wrist temperature,8 and sleep duration.

      Get the inside track on your cycle.

      AppleWatch has innovative sensors that track your temperature while you sleep.8 CycleTracking uses this data to provide a retrospective estimate of when you have likely ovulated, which can be helpful for family planning.9 Wrist temperature can also be used to improve periodpredictions.

      Fitness

      Miles of motivation.

      Close your rings.

      Three rings show all the ways youre active and help you reach your Move, Exercise, and Stand goals everyday.

      Works for all your workouts.

      From running to HIIT to yoga, you have a healthy number of ways to work out with accurate tracking of all the metrics you most want tosee.

      Hit the ground running.

      AppleWatch has everything you need for running. You can use views like Heart Rate Zones, target specific time goals with Pacer, or do interval training with CustomWorkouts.

      Go above, below, and beyond.

      AppleWatch is water resistant,10 dust resistant,11 and crack resistant. So you can swim, hike, and do everything in between without aworry.

      Safety

      Keep help close at hand.

      Emergency services when you need them.

      Apple Watch can detect if you take a hard fall or are in a severe crash and then automatically help connect you with help, share your location, and notify your emergencycontacts.12

      Peace of mind on your wrist.

      CheckIn notifies someone when you arrive at a destination. If youre going for a run after dark, you can start a CheckIn for the duration of your workout to automatically notify a friend when youredone.

      Find My. What a find.

      The Find My app helps you easily find a friend in a crowded place or share your location to stay connected. You can also locate your lost or stolendevices.

      Sound the alarm.

      With Siren on Apple Watch Ultra, you can quickly alert those around you when you need help. Just press and hold the Action button and wait for thecountdown.
