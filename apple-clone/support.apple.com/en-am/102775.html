    Countries and regions that support Apple Pay

    Apple Pay offers an easy, secure, and private way to pay on iPhone, iPad, Apple Watch, Mac, and Apple Vision Pro.

    Apple Pay is accepted anywhere you see these symbols and marks in stores, within apps, and on the web in Safari and third-party browsers.1 You can add your credit, debit, or prepaid cards from participating banks and card issuers in these countries and regions:
