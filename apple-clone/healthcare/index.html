    IT and Security

    Simple and secure. At any scale.

    Apple fits within your existing infrastructure, so you can set up, manage, and secure any number of devices with ease.

    • Zero-Touch Deployment

      Setup in a snap.

      Zero-Touch Deployment

      Setup in a snap.

      Apple Business Manager works with MDM solutions to make deployment and management exceptionally easy. Automated Device Enrollment allows your IT team to configure devices remotely and customize the setup process so Apple devices are ready to go right out of the box.

    • Device Management

      Keep it all under control.

      Device Management

      Keep it all under control.

      Apple devices come with built-in management and security frameworks that make it easy for IT to configure and manage devices, buy and distribute apps, and monitor threats remotely. Whether you have 10 devices or 10,000, IT can easily create profiles to ensure that employees have everything they need to be secure and productive.

    • Hardware and Software

      Secure by design.

      Hardware and Software

      Secure by design.

      Apple hardware and software aredesigned for maximum security. Whether your clinicians are onMac, iPad, or iPhone, Apple siliconand operating systems worktogether to deliver powerful features that keep your organizations data anddevices safe.

    • Touch ID and Face ID

      One touch and youre in.

      Touch ID and Face ID

      One touch and youre in.

      TouchID and FaceID give users afast,easy, and secureway to unlock devices, open protected files, and retrieve passkeys at a touch or a glance. So your clinicians wont waste time looking up forgotten passwords.
