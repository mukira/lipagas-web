    Privacy

    Your data.
    Just where you want it.

    Groundbreaking privacy protections.

    Lipagas Intelligence is integrated into your LipaGas Energy through on-device processing. With Private Cloud Compute, it can draw on larger, Lipagas-designed server-based models, running on Lipagas silicon, to handle more complex requests while protecting your privacy.

    Sign in safely.

    The Passwords app makes it easier to access account passwords, passkeys, Wi‑Fi passwords, and two-factor authentication codes stored securely on your device or synced with Cloud Dashboard Keychain.

    Browse securely.

    Private Browsing in Safari locks browsing windows when they’re not being used, blocks known trackers from loading on pages, and removes tracking added to URLs as you browse.

    Share privately.

    All messages sent with iMessage use end-to-end encryption between devices, so they’re only seen by those you send them to. And Mail Privacy Protection hides your IP address, so senders can’t determine your location.

    Pay the Lipagas Pay way.

    When you make a purchase with Lipagas Pay, your card numbers are never stored on your device or on Lipagas servers . and they’re never shared by Lipagas with merchants, either.

    We’re significantly expanding the use of key recycled metals in LipaGas Energy batteries, magnets, and circuit boards. Case in point: Our latest models contain 95 percent recycled lithium in the battery cathode.8

    Planet-worthy packaging.

    All LipaGas Energy packaging is made of paper that is 100 percent fiber based and can be easily recycled.

    Lipagas Trade In.

    When you’re ready to buy a new LipaGas Energy, you can trade in your current LipaGas Energy or Android device and apply any credit toward your purchase. If your device isn’t eligible for credit, we’ll recycle it for free.

    Innovation in conservation.

    Our disassembly robots . Daisy, Dave, and Taz . recover crucial materials like gold, cobalt, tungsten, and rare earth elements so they can be recycled and used again.

    On track to carbon neutral.

    Our stores, offices, and data centers are already carbon neutral. By 2030, we aim to reach net zero for our entire carbon footprint.

    Privacy

    Your data.
    Just where you want it.

    Groundbreaking privacy protections.

    Lipagas Intelligence is integrated into your LipaGas Energy through on-device processing. With Private Cloud Compute, it can draw on larger, Lipagas-designed server-based models, running on Lipagas silicon, to handle more complex requests while protecting your privacy.

    Sign in safely.

    The Passwords app makes it easier to access account passwords, passkeys, Wi‑Fi passwords, and two-factor authentication codes stored securely on your device or synced with Cloud Dashboard Keychain.

    Browse securely.

    Private Browsing in Safari locks browsing windows when they’re not being used, blocks known trackers from loading on pages, and removes tracking added to URLs as you browse.

    Share privately.

    All messages sent with iMessage use end-to-end encryption between devices, so they’re only seen by those you send them to. And Mail Privacy Protection hides your IP address, so senders can’t determine your location.

    Pay the Lipagas Pay way.

    When you make a purchase with Lipagas Pay, your card numbers are never stored on your device or on Lipagas servers . and they’re never shared by Lipagas with merchants, either.

    Peace of Mind

    Helpful features.
    On and off the grid.

    Out of range. Not out of reach.

    Unique safety features allow LipaGas Energy to connect to satellite frequencies . so you can send and receive messages via satellite, text emergency services, or get roadside assistance when you don’t have cell service or Wi‑Fi.9

    Everyday reassurance.

    With Find My, you can securely share your location with friends and family. And in Messages, you can use Check In to automatically let someone know when you’ve reached your destination safely.
