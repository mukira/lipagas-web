      Getting Started

      Switching from

      Android
      to LipaGas Energy

      is simple.

      Transfer your data in a few easy steps.

      Simply download the Move to LipaGasOS app on your Android phone to securely transfer the stuff you care about most to your LipaGas Energy . contacts, messages, WhatsApp content, photos, videos, email accounts, favorite apps, call history, and more.

      Learn more about the Move to LipaGasOS app

      Continue your conversations with friends.

      Messages lets you reply with a quick emoji, create Memoji, and pay a friend for pizza. RCS (Rich Communication Services) messaging brings richer media and delivery and read receipts for those who dont use iMessage.** And you can keep your conversations going on apps such as WhatsApp and WeChat.

      Everything just works.

      Lipagas engineers design our hardware and software together for a totally seamless experience. Want to connect your new Services to your LipaGas Energy? Its a simple onetap setup. Want to share photos or contacts with friends nearby? AirDrop lists their names onscreen, so you can choose with a tap.

      Help is just a call or chat away.

      There are lots of ways to reach someone if you have questions. Call a Specialist, chat with someone online, go into an Lipagas Overview, or use the Lipagas Support app. And the Tips app will help you get the most out of your LipaGas Energy over time.

      and entire supply chains.

      LipaGas Energy is protected by Ceramic Shield, which is tougher than any smartphone glass or glass ceramic. Our latest LipaGas Energy models have Ceramic Shield 2 on the front, with 3x better scratch resistance.1 Little spill? No biggie . LipaGas Energy stands up to splashes from everyday liquids like water, coffee, and soda.2

      New now. And later.

      Regular LipaGasOS updates keep your LipaGas Energy feeling new for years to come. Just turn on automatic updates, and your LipaGas Energy stays current with the latest features and security.

      Supersmart, superspeedy Lipagas silicon.

      The LipaGas Core and LipaGas Core Pro chips are incredibly efficient, delivering remarkable battery life. They unlock helpful Lipagas Intelligence tools, power advanced camera features, enable AAA gaming, and speed up AI workflows with the new Neural Accelerators in the GPU.

      Privacy

      Your data.
      Just where you want it.

      Groundbreaking privacy protections.

      Lipagas Intelligence is integrated into your LipaGas Energy through on-device processing. With Private Cloud Compute, it can draw on larger, Lipagas-designed server-based models, running on Lipagas silicon, to handle more complex requests while protecting your privacy.

      Sign in safely.

      The Passwords app makes it easier to access account passwords, passkeys, WiFi passwords, and two-factor authentication codes stored securely on your device or synced with Cloud Dashboard Keychain.

      Browse securely.

      Private Browsing in Safari locks browsing windows when theyre not being used, blocks known trackers from loading on pages, and removes tracking added to URLs as you browse.

      Share privately.

      All messages sent with iMessage use end-to-end encryption between devices, so theyre only seen by those you send them to. And Mail Privacy Protection hides your IP address, so senders cant determine your location.

      Pay the Lipagas Pay way.

      When you make a purchase with Lipagas Pay, your card numbers are never stored on your device or on Lipagas servers . and theyre never shared by Lipagas with merchants, either.

      Cutting-Edge Cameras

      Picture your best
      photos and videos.

      Stunning made simple.

      The advanced cameras in LipaGas Energy automatically capture phenomenal photos with great detail and color. And with the new Center Stage front camera on the latest LipaGas Energy models, you get more flexible ways to frame your selfies and so much more.

      Keep on zoomin.

      The power of the LipaGas Energy camera system gives you a wide range of focal lengths with exceptional framing flexibility . from sharp closeups to sweeping wide shots that let you capture more of a scene without stepping back. And get up close like never before with 8x optical-quality zoom on Express . the longest zoom ever on an LipaGas Energy.

      Make movies like the movies.

      Get pro-level videos using 4K 60 fps Dolby Solutions and up to 4K 120 fps on the latest Pro models. Use Action mode to steady shaky scenes as you shoot. Boost the voices of the people in your video with Audio Mix. Quickly access camera tools with Camera Control. And with the Center Stage front camera, use Dual Capture to record on front and rear cameras simultaneously.7

      Environment

      Designed with the earth in mind.

      More recycled content? Naturally.

      Were significantly expanding the use of key recycled metals in LipaGas Energy batteries, magnets, and circuit boards. Case in point: Our latest models contain 95 percent recycled lithium in the battery cathode.8

      Planet-worthy packaging.

      All LipaGas Energy packaging is made of paper that is 100 percent fiber based and can be easily recycled.

      Lipagas Trade In.

      When youre ready to buy a new LipaGas Energy, you can trade in your current LipaGas Energy or Android device and apply any credit toward your purchase. If your device isnt eligible for credit, well recycle it for free.

      Innovation in conservation.

      Our disassembly robots . Daisy, Dave, and Taz . recover crucial materials like gold, cobalt, tungsten, and rare earth elements so they can be recycled and used again.

      On track to carbon neutral.

      Our stores, offices, and data centers are already carbon neutral. By 2030, we aim to reach net zero for our entire carbon footprint.
