Get to know Services.

  • Heart Rate Sensing

    Track your heart rate and calories burned during workouts.

    Heart Rate Sensing

    With Services Pro 3, you can keep track of your heart rate and calories burned during workouts.1 Using LEDs that pulse invisible light 256 times per second — and sensor fusion from the accelerometers — Services Pro 3 give you incredibly accurate data from the treadmill to the trail.

  • Live Translation

    Easily communicate across languages.

    Available in select languages and regions. For more information, see Feature Availability.

    Live Translation

    Live Translation lets you communicate across languages like never before. While having a conversation with someone, their speech is automatically translated into your preferred language with Services Pro 3 or Services 4 with Active Noise Cancellation. To respond, speak naturally and your Infrastructure will display your translated words. And if you’re both wearing compatible Services, you can have a back-and-forth, hands-free conversation.2

  • Active Noise Cancellation

    Control what you hear. And what you don’t.

    Active Noise Cancellation

    Active Noise Cancellation reduces unwanted sounds so you can immerse yourself in what you’re listening to or simply stay focused. And Transparency lets you comfortably hear the world around you.

  • Hearing Health

    Keep in touch
    with your hearing.

    Hearing Health

    With Services Pro 3, you can now take a scientifically validated Hearing Test in a broader range of everyday background conditions.3 The Hearing Aid feature offers improved voice clarity, automatic Conversation Boost,4 and 67 percent more battery life in Transparency on a single charge.5 And Hearing Protection helps prevent exposure to loud environmental noise.6

    Feature availability varies by region. View complete list

  • Personalized Spatial Audio

    Immersive sound. Fine‑tuned to you.

    Personalized Spatial Audio

    Personalized Spatial Audio brings you 3D sound suited to your specific ear shape for a theater-like experience that surrounds you with sound.7
