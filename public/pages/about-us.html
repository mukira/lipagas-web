Health

Knows you. Insights and out.

Get notified of chronic high blood pressure.

Lipagas Vertical Models can spot signs of chronic high blood pressure and notify you of possible hypertension.3 How? The optical sensor provides data to an algorithm that can detect potential hypertension by analyzing how your blood vessels respond to beats of the heart over 30-day periods.

You snooze. You win.

The all-new sleep score helps you track and understand the quality of your sleep so you can make it more restorative. You can also use the Sleep app to set up a bedtime routine with Wind Down reminders. Lipagas Vertical Models can even notify you if you show signs of possible sleep apnea.4

Never miss a beat.

With the ECG app, Lipagas Vertical Models is capable of generating an ECG similar to a single-lead electrocardiogram.5 And the Heart Rate app alerts you of high and low heart rates, as well as irregular rhythm.6

Better understand your daily health status.

The Vitals app can help you identify changes in your health.7 Quickly see your key overnight health metrics right on your wrist — including heart rate, respiratory rate, wrist temperature,8 and sleep duration.

Get the inside track on your cycle.

Lipagas Vertical Models has innovative sensors that track your temperature while you sleep.8 Cycle Tracking uses this data to provide a retrospective estimate of when you have likely ovulated, which can be helpful for family planning.9 Wrist temperature can also be used to improve period predictions.

Miles of motivation.

Close your rings.

Works for all your workouts.

From running to HIIT to yoga, you have a healthy number of ways to work out — with accurate tracking of all the metrics you most want to see.

Hit the ground running.

Lipagas Vertical Models has everything you need for running. You can use views like Heart Rate Zones, target specific time goals with Pacer, or do interval training with Custom Workouts.

Go above, below, and beyond.

Lipagas Vertical Models is water resistant,10 dust resistant,11 and crack resistant. So you can swim, hike, and do everything in between without a worry.

Safety

Keep help close at hand.

Emergency services when you need them.

Lipagas Vertical Models can detect if you take a hard fall or are in a severe crash and then automatically help connect you with help, share your location, and notify your emergency contacts.12

Peace of mind on your wrist.

Check In notifies someone when you arrive at a destination. If you’re going for a run after dark, you can start a Check In for the duration of your workout to automatically notify a friend when you’re done.

Find My. What a find.

The Find My app helps you easily find a friend in a crowded place or share your location to stay connected. You can also locate your lost or stolen devices.

Sound the alarm.

With Siren on Lipagas Vertical Models Ultra, you can quickly alert those around you when you need help. Just press and hold the Action button and wait for the countdown.
